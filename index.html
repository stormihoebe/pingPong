About Ping-Pong

Ping-Pong is a counting game that returns numbers and "ping" or "pong" responses. Enter a number to begin playing.

Rules of Ping-Pongs

Ping-pong will count from 1 to any number you choose with the following exceptions:

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 will be replaced with "ping"
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 will be replaced with "pong"
  3. Numbers divisible by 3 and 5 will be replaced with "ping-pong"
